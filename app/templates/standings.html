The Crypt

A New Descent

"I have been busy. Below the sub-basement, past the wet corridor where the rats refuse to go, I have unsealed a door that has not been opened in a very long time. Behind it: The Crypt. Ten waves. Ten. Each one worse than the last. My personal collection — pawns, knights, bishops, rooks, and worse — will crawl up through the grates to destroy you. You will start with a king, a queen, and three trembling pawns. It costs five rating points to enter. Five points pressed into the dark like a coin into a dead man's palm. Survive three waves and you break even. Survive six and real money starts to accumulate. Survive nine and you may cash out with twenty-five points. Or... you face ME. Wave ten. The boss wave. Beat me — and I will give you FIFTY rating points from my own bleeding ledger. At each milestone, you choose: take what you've earned and crawl back up the stairs... or push deeper. Three attempts per day. The crypt opens at midnight and closes at midnight. I keep the ledger. I keep the score. I keep everything. Come down. If you dare."

The Crypt — 10-wave solo gauntlet. Costs 5 rating points to enter. Cash out at milestones (waves 3, 6, 9) or risk it all against Enoch at wave 10 for +50 points. 3 runs per day. Safety nets lock in your gains at each milestone.
