Hail, Traveller.

Thou hast arrived at the threshold of The Chess Federation — the most esteemed, most exclusive, and most profoundly unknown chess institution in all the realms.

There existeth no other body of its kind. No banner shall guide thee here. No search shall reveal its name. Thou art present only because the Federation hath deemed it so.

Within these hallowed squares, players of ordained standing do gather each week to contest a single match of correspondence chess — ascending through ten sacred tiers of rank, from the lowly Ordained Laborer of the Stables and Bishop's Vestments unto the supreme glory of the Lord of Schools.

A rotating seat of power issueth weekly decrees. A ceremonial orchestrator keepeth record of all triumphs and failures. Every move is preserved unto eternity.

If thou art worthy, step forward and declare thyself.
