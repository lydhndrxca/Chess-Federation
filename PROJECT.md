# Chess Federation

A web application for the Chess Federation — a private fellowship of chess players governed by an ancient hierarchy of ten ranks.

## Purpose

- Display the Sacred Hierarchy of the Federation's ten ordained ranks.
- Track players, their biographies, win/loss records, and rank progression.
- Present the recovered chronicles and lore of the Federation.
- Provide an administrative interface for recording match results and managing members.

## Status

Under initial development.
